Privacy Policy

Game of Life – Survival (iOS)

Effective Date:

This Privacy Policy explains how the iOS app Game of Life – Survival and the accompanying website seedtovoid.com handle information. We keep this simple: we do not collect personal data, and we do not share data with third parties.

1) Scope

This policy applies to the iOS app Game of Life – Survival and to gameplay services hosted at seedtovoid.com, including the public leaderboard API.

2) Information We Collect

  • Personal data: We do not collect any personal information. No accounts, no login, no analytics SDKs, no trackers.
  • Game data you choose to submit: If you submit a score to the public leaderboard, we store the data you provide with the score (e.g., a display name, score value, difficulty, and basic gameplay stats). Do not use a real name if you prefer not to.

3) How We Use Data

Submitted scores are used only to display and maintain the public leaderboard. We do not profile users and we do not use the data for advertising or marketing.

4) Third‑Party Sharing

We do not sell or share data with third parties. The app and API do not include third‑party analytics or advertising SDKs.

5) Data Retention

Leaderboard entries remain visible until they are rotated out by newer scores or removed upon request. If you would like a submitted score removed, contact us and include enough detail to identify the entry (display name, approximate date/time, difficulty, and score).

6) Children’s Privacy

The app does not collect personal information from anyone, including children. If you believe a child has submitted identifiable information to the leaderboard, contact us for removal.

7) Security

Connections to the API use HTTPS. While we do not collect personal data, we still take reasonable steps to safeguard submitted leaderboard entries.

8) Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
